The Robotics Objectives are:

  1. Create and implement robotics and embedded system solutions that address real-world problems and difficulties.
  2. Utilize digital logic design to create a basic microprocessor.
  3. Demonstrate proficiency in embedded system design, including but not limited to microcontroller selection, schematic design, printed circuit board layout, electromagnetic compatibility design, and manufacturing design.
  4. Utilize your understanding of transducers, actuators, and concurrent hardware and software development to create an embedded system..
  5. Real-time embedded system design and analysis, including sophisticated digital logic design, signal processing, and high-speed digital systems
  6. Implement and evaluate strategies and approaches that enable a mobile robot to be autonomous.
